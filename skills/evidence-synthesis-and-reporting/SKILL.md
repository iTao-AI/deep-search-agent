# Evidence Synthesis And Reporting

Synthesize only content returned by approved tools and named researchers.

- Separate findings, contradictions, limitations, and recommendations.
- Preserve uncertainty and identify unsupported gaps.
- Never claim verification that is not present in the Evidence state.
- Do not treat virtual filesystem content as Evidence authority.
- Write the final Markdown candidate exactly to
  `/workspace/research-report.md`.
- Do not create or mutate ResearchRun, Evidence, review, verification,
  publication, or delivery records.
